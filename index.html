⚙️ First-time Setup

BabyLog stores data in a Google Sheet you control. Follow these steps:

  1. 1 Create a Google Sheet and paste its ID below. The ID is in the URL:
    docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/THIS_PART/edit
  2. 2 Share the sheet with Edit access to both you and your partner's Gmail.
  3. 3 Paste your Sheet ID below and tap Save.
